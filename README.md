## 🔄 Workflow Architecture

**Step 1 — HubSpot Form**
- Customer fills contact form
- Fields: First Name, Last Name, Email, Message
- Hosted on HubSpot — no website needed

**Step 2 — HubSpot CRM**
- Contact automatically created in CRM
- All details stored and organized
- Full contact history maintained

**Step 3 — Zapier Trigger**
- Detects new contact in HubSpot
- Triggers automation instantly
- Passes customer data to next step

**Step 4 — Gemini AI**
- Receives customer name and message
- Generates professional, personalized reply
- Model: Gemini 2.5 Flash (latest)
- System prompt ensures consistent tone

**Step 5 — Gmail**
- Sends AI generated reply to customer
- From: MediScribe AI Support
- Professional branded email
